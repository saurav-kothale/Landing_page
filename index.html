Chai Shotts Evolution

The Chai Shotts Cafe began in December 2019, serving over 1,000 takeaway customers. In 2020, we opened our first cafe branch and distributed food packets to those in need during the pandemic. Founded by Mrs. Jinalba Apurvsinh Thakor, a Bhavnagar University postgraduate, and led by co-founder and CEO Mr. Apurvsinh Jitendrasinh Thakor, we’ve become a renowned brand in the Indian food industry. With a vision to create entrepreneurs and offer premium chai, The Chai Shotts Cafe sells franchise models across India.

Franchise Benefits

The Chai Shotts Cafe offers extensive support to franchise partners, including raw material supply, cafe kitchen equipment, and brand name usage. We provide a startup franchise kit, menu customization, and handle FSSAI registration. Assistance with cafe decor and layout is also included. Comprehensive training for owners and staff, a maintenance planner, and local marketing action plans are part of our services. We support digital marketing, food blogging, and onboarding with Zomato, Swiggy, Google, and Map services. A dedicated franchise helpline and ongoing staff support ensure your success.
